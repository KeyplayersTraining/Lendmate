Platform Basics

What Blu is, what it is built on, and how to navigate it as a VA.

What is Blu
Primary Purpose
Blu (BluMortgage) is a CRM and workflow automation platform specifically built for mortgage professionals. It manages leads, contacts, deals, and automates follow-up communications. Unlike Filogix or Finmo, Blu's primary strength is pipeline visibility and automated workflows — not direct lender submission.
CRM + automation — not a submission platform
What it is Built On
Blu is built on top of Salesforce — the world's most widely used CRM platform. This means Blu inherits Salesforce's structure for contacts, deals, and automation rules. If you have used Salesforce before, Blu will feel familiar. If not, the core concepts are contacts, deals, and pipelines.
Built on Salesforce — know this fact
Key Navigation Tabs
Deals tab: Shows all active deals — this is your primary working area as a VA. Contacts: All leads, borrowers, and referral partners. Tasks: Pending actions and follow-ups assigned to you or auto-generated. Campaigns: Marketing automations — typically managed by the broker, not VAs.

Contacts & Automation

How leads enter Blu, what triggers automations, and what the pipeline stages mean.

What Triggers Automations
The Key Trigger: Deal Stage Change
In Blu, automations are triggered by deal stage changes — not by the lead's income bracket, number of dependents, or time of day the lead was added. When a deal moves from one stage to the next, Blu automatically fires the actions set up for that stage: emails, tasks, reminders, and internal notifications.
Pipeline Stages
1
New Lead
Lead has entered the system — initial contact not yet made
2
Documents Collected
Borrower has provided all required documentation
3
Approval
Conditional approval received from lender
4
Closed Won
Mortgage has funded successfully
5
Closed Lost
Deal did not proceed — borrower withdrew or declined
What "Closed Won" Means
"Closed Won" in Blu means the mortgage has been funded successfully. The deal is complete. The borrower has received their funds and the transaction has closed at the lawyer's office. Do not mark a deal Closed Won until funding is confirmed.
Closed Won = funded. Not approved, not signed.
Automation Workflows
Blu Automation Workflows automatically send emails, create tasks, and set reminders when a deal stage changes. They can also update deal stages automatically based on rules. This removes manual follow-up gaps. As a VA, you execute tasks created by automations — you do not build or edit automations without broker permission.
Never edit automations without broker authorization

Deal Management

How to create deals, what information is required, and what Blu can automate within deals.

Creating a New Deal
What to Enter First
When creating a new deal, the first required fields are deal name and associated contact(s). The deal name is typically the borrower's name or file reference. You must link at least one contact (the primary borrower) before the deal can be saved.
Deal name + linked contact = minimum to save
What Blu Can Auto-Update
Blu can automatically update deal stage progression based on workflow rules — for example, moving a deal from "Documents Collected" to "Approval" when certain conditions are met. It cannot calculate mortgage penalties, update birthdays, or calculate land transfer taxes automatically.
Automation handles stage changes — not mortgage math

Integrations & Communication

How Blu connects to other tools and how communication works inside the CRM.

Key Integrations
Finmo Integration
The most common tool that integrates with Blu is Finmo — the mortgage application and document collection platform. This integration allows deals in Blu to connect to applications in Finmo, keeping the CRM and the mortgage application in sync. TurboTax, Canva, and Zillow are not Blu integrations.
Blu + Finmo = the standard integration stack
Email Integration
For automations that must reliably send emails from within Blu, use a connected email integration — Office 365 sync or Gmail sync. Personal email accounts or the broker's direct Gmail are not suitable for automation delivery. Always use the connected integration to ensure emails send correctly.
Personal Gmail ≠ automation email — use integration
Text Messaging in Blu
Blu's text messaging integration allows VAs and brokers to send SMS messages to borrowers directly from inside the CRM. All messages are logged in the deal record, creating a full communication audit trail. This is not mass marketing spam — it is one-to-one communication tied to a specific deal. Use it professionally and purposefully.
All Blu texts are logged — treat them as formal communication

Compliance & Data Handling

Why logging communication matters and what data must be protected.

Communication Logging
Why You Must Log All Communication
Every borrower communication — calls, emails, texts, and in-person conversations — must be logged in Blu. This creates a full audit trail that meets compliance requirements. If a compliance issue arises and the communication is not logged, it effectively did not happen from a legal standpoint. Log immediately after each interaction.
Data Protection
Most Sensitive Information
A borrower's credit and financial information — income, debts, credit score, banking details — is the most sensitive category of data in Blu. This data must be stored securely and never shared outside the platform without explicit broker authorization. A postal code or middle name does not carry the same protection level.
Financial + credit data = highest protection level
Who Can Edit Automations
Only Admins or team members explicitly granted permission can edit automation workflows in Blu. Real estate agents, referral partners, and borrowers do not have this access. As a VA, you work within automations — you do not change them. If an automation is broken or behaving incorrectly, escalate to the broker.
Automation edits = broker/Admin only
